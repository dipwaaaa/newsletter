The Short n' Sweet Tour is the ongoing fifth concert tour and first arena tour by American singer Sabrina Carpenter, in support of her sixth studio album, Short n' Sweet. The tour began on September 23, 2024, in Columbus, United States, and is scheduled to conclude on March 26, 2025, in Milan, Italy.

The emails i can't send Tour is Sabrina Carpenter's fourth headlining tour, in promotion of her fifth studio album Emails I Can't Send. The tour took place in North America, South America, Europe and Asia, starting September 29, 2022 and ending on August 4, 2023.

The Singular Tour was Sabrina Carpenter's third headlining tour, in promotion of her third and fourth studio albums Singular: Act I and Singular: Act II. The tour took place in North America and Asia, starting March 2, 2019 and ending on April 11, 2019.
